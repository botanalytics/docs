# Switch Viewing Analytics for Different Projects

## Method 1: Switching Between Projects from the Account Dashboard

### Go to the Active Project Dropdown

- Click on the name of the currently active project located in the top left corner of the account dashboard.
  <img src={DashboardActiveProject} alt="Dashboard Active Project" />

### List Projects

- A drop-down menu will appear showing a list of available projects.
  <img src={SwitchDropdownMenu} alt="Switch Dropdown Menu" />

### Select the Project to Switch To

- Click on the project name you want to switch to from the list.
  <img src={SwitchSelectProject} alt="Switch Select Project" />

### Viewing the Chosen Project

- You will be taken to the selected project's analytics, allowing you to access its details and features.
  <img src={SwitchViewProject} alt="Switch View Project" />

## Method 2: Switching Between Projects from the Projects Dashboard

### Access the Projects Dashboard

- Click on the Botanalytics logo positioned at the top left corner of the account dashboard. You will be directed to the projects dashboard, where you can manage your projects.
  <img src={ProjectClickLogo} alt="Project Click Logo" />

### Locate the Project to Switch To

- In the projects dashboard, locate the project you wish to switch to, click on anywhere in the project tile.
  <img src={SwitchClickTile} alt="Switch Click Tile" />

### Viewing the Chosen Project

- You will be taken to the selected project's analytics, allowing you to access its details and features.
  <img src={SwitchViewProject} alt="Switch View Project" />
