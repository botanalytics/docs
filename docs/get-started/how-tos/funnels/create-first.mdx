# Create Your first Funnel

### Navigate to Funnels Dashboard
- Navigate to the "Behaviors" tab.
- Locate and click on the "Funnels" button in the navigation menu.
  <img src={FunnelsDashboard} alt="Funnels Dashboard" />

### Add Funnel

- Click on the "Add Funnel" button on the funnel page.
  <img src={FunnelClickonAddFunnel} alt="Funnel Click on Add Funnel" />

### Adding Funnel Name

- You'll be directed to a pop-up page where you can name your funnel and add steps for the funnel.
  <img src={FunnelClickonName} alt="Funnel Click on name" />

### Adding Funnel Steps

- After naming the funnel, proceed to add steps to the funnel.
- Choose either built-in steps from the predefined list or define custom steps tailored to your needs, and set conditions for each step.
  <img src={FunnelClicktoAddSteps} alt="Funnel Click to Add Steps" />

### Add Funnel

- Once you've defined the steps, click on the "Add" button to complete the process of adding your funnel.
  <img src={FunnelClicktoAdd} alt="Funnel Click to Add" />

### Funnel Displayed In the Dashboard

- Your funnel will be displayed on the Funnels dashboard.
  <img src={FunnelView} alt="Funnel View" />
