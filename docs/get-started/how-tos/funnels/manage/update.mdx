# Update a Funnel

### Navigate to Funnels Dashboard

- Navigate to the "Behaviors" tab.
- Locate and click on the "Funnels" button in the navigation menu.
  <img src={FunnelsDashboard} alt="Funnels Dashboard" />

### Editing the Funnel

- Click on the ellipsis (three dots) in the top right corner of the funnel.
  <img src={FunnelsClickonEllipsis} alt="Funnels Click on Ellipsis" />

### Select "Edit" to Update the Funnel

- A dropdown menu will be revealed. Click on "Edit" from the dropdown menu to open the edit window.
  <img src={FunnelsClickonEdit} alt="Funnels Click on Edit" />

### Making Changes to the Funnel

- Inside the edit window, you can make all the desired changes to the funnel.
- You can choose to update the name by clicking on the name tile with the name of the funnel.
  <img src={FunnelsClicktoUpdatename} alt="Funnels Click to Update name" />
- You can also choose to add a step to the funnel by clicking on "Add Step" to reveal a dropdown menu.
  <img src={FunnelsClicktoAddNewStep} alt="Funnels Click to Add New Step" />
  <img src={FunnelsDropdown} alt="Funnels Dropdown" />

### Finalizing the Changes

- After making the changes, click on the "Update" button. This action will finalize the modifications you've made to the funnel.
  <img src={FunnelsClickOnUpdate} alt="Funnels Click On Update" />

### Viewing the Updated Funnel

- After updating, you will be able to view your updated funnel in the funnel dashboard.
  <img src={FunnelsUpdatedView} alt="Funnel Updated View" />
