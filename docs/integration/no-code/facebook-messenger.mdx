### Facebook Messenger

#### Access Your Account Dashboard

- Log in to your Botanalytics account using your credentials. Once logged in, click on the Botanalytics logo in the top left corner to access the dashboard.
  <img src={ProjectClickLogo} alt="Project Click Logo" />

#### Navigate to Channel Settings

- In the dashboard, locate the three dots (ellipsis) icon in the top right corner of the project you wish to add Facebook Integration to. Click on it to display a drop-down menu.
  <img src={ProjectClickEllipsis} alt="Project Click Ellipsis" />

- From the drop-down menu, select "Settings."
  <img src={ProjectClickSettings} alt="Project Click Settings" />

#### Navigate to "Channels" Section

- In the settings window, click on "Channels" to manage your connected channels.
  <img src={ChannelsChannelsTab} alt="ChannelsChannelsTab" />

#### Connect New Channel

- Under the "Channels" section, click on the "Connect New Channel" button.
    <img src={ChannelConnectNew} alt="Channel Connect New" />

### Scenario 1: Logged into Botanalytics with Facebook Account

#### Select Facebook Integration

- A new window will appear, presenting a list of available channels. Select "Messenger" to initiate the one-click integration process.
  <img src={FacebookDirectChannel} alt="Facebook Messenger Direct Channel" />

#### Connect With Facebook

- In the pop-up window, click on "Connect with Facebook" to connect your account.
  <img src={ConnectWithFacebook} alt="Connect With Facebook" />

#### Authorize Access

- If you logged into Botanalytics with your Facebook account, you will be asked to continue with that account. Click on "Continue" in the account settings to proceed.
  <img src={FacebookAuthorize} alt="Facebook Authorize" />

#### Choose the Page

- After authorizing access, you can choose the Facebook page you want to connect to Botanalytics.
  <img src={FacebookChoosePages} alt="Facebook Choose Pages" />

#### Allow Botanalytics permission

- In the next page, you will be asked to allow Botanalytics's app for permission. Click on the permissions and click on done to go to the next page.
  <img src={FacebookPermissions} alt="Facebook Permissions" />

#### Confirmation Message

- In the next page, there will be a confirmation message, click on "OK" to go to the next step.
  <img src={FacebookConfirmationOk} alt="Facebook Authorization Confirmation OK" />

#### Select the Page You Want To Connect With

- From the dropdown "Which page would you like to connect with" shown below. Select the Facebook Messenger page from the list of the pages.
  <img src={FacebookSelectPageDropdown} alt="Facebook Page Select Dropdown" />
  <img src={FacebookSelectWantedPage} alt="Facebook Select Wanted Page" />

#### Adding One Click Facebook Messenger Channel

- Click on add channel to add the channel.
  <img src={FacebookAddOneClick} alt="Add One Facebook Messenger Channel" />

#### View Channel

- You can now view the new channel in the Channel Dashboard.
  <img src={FacebookViewConnectedChannel} alt="View One Click Facebook Messenger Channel" />
